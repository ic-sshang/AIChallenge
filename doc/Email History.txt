Overview

This document describes the structure and purpose of the following classes, which represent different types of email notification records in the system:
•	EmailHistory
1. EmailHistory
Purpose:
Represents a record of general email notifications sent to customers, including status and tracking information.
Available Data:
•	EmailRecipient: The recipient's email address.
•	EmailType: Enum indicating the type of email sent.
•	EmailTypeDesc: Description of the email type.
•	EmailSentDate: Date and time the email was sent.
•	EmailStatus: Status of the email (e.g., sent, failed).
•	CustomerId: ID of the customer associated with the email.
•	CustomerName: Name of the customer.
•	AccountNumber: Customer's account number.
•	Customer: Navigation property to the related Customer entity.
