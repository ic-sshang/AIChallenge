Identified Technical Problems
Email update behaviors are inconsistent across software components. There are many endpoints to update emails all with cascading impacts.
Email updates can retain or expose old payment methods, depending on how unregistering or ownership change is handled
Invoice imports update emails differently depending on CRM options (e.g., Collier example with Biller Option 108)
The Customer Import Tool does not respect multi-user rules: (1) Emails are updated without proper validation of account ownership roles (2) There is no easy way to import new email addresses without breaking things (3) Marketing does not have a tool for their Paperless, AutoPay or Adoption campaigns
Biller Portal email changes can unintentionally reassigning ownership or unlinking accounts
CRM options can update emails with unintended impacts
