The Business Logic Layer contains the core functionality and business rules of the application. 
Business Rules
:
Implements the business logic and rules.
Ensures data integrity and consistency.
Data Processing
:
Processes data received from the Presentation Layer.
Applies business rules and logic to the data.
Communication
:
Interacts with the Data Access Layer to retrieve and store data.
Sends processed data back to the Presentation Layer.
Validation
:
Validates data before processing or storing it.
Ensures that business rules are adhered to.
