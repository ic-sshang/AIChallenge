The Statement of Work (SOW) Waiver Request Form is used to formally request an exemption from the standard SOW billing process for a specific project. This form is intended for use by functional leads (VP or higher) who have determined that a full SOW is not feasible or necessary under specific circumstances. If you need to request an SOW Waiver, follow the steps below to ensure your submission is complete and routed for approval efficiently. 
‚úÖ When to Use This Form
Use this form when you wish to waive or reduce the cost of an SOW for a specific Services project, including: 
CIS Platform Conversions/Upgrade
Invoice Presentment Changes
SSO/Engagement Portal Enablement
Please note that a waiver of more than 50% of the original SOW amount must also be approved by the Vice-President of Implementations and/or the Chief Customer Officer.
