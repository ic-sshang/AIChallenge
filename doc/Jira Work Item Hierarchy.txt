Summary
The purpose of this document is to outline and define the Jira Work Items and their hierarchy.
Item Type
Definition
Purpose
Examples
Theme
(highest-level strategic goal)
A large focus area or business objective that spans quarters or even years.
Helps align multiple initiatives under a shared strategic direction
“Improve Customer Experience,” “Expand to New Markets.”
Initiative
A large body of work that aligns to a theme and is composed of multiple epics.
Represents a major program, objective, or feature set that takes multiple teams or sprints to deliver.
Initiatives roll up to Themes and down into Epics.
Epic
A large feature or body of work that can be broken down into multiple stories or tasks.
Organizes related user stories under one functional deliverable.
“User Authentication,” “Checkout Flow Redesign.”
