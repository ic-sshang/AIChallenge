Overview
Stakeholders and collaborators overseeing and ensuring quality, consistency, and alignment of enterprise architectural decisions.
Participants 
Architects
Engineering Leadership
Product Leadership
Objectives
Consensus within the organization that a change is required
Confirm the organization business goals and strategic drivers
Define an architectural vision
Build consensus in favor of the vision within the organization
Define scope of the architecture effort and constraints 
Inputs
Business goals
Strategic plans
Mission statement
Financial constraints
Time limits
Current business system description
Outputs
Business requirements and key systems and architect drivers
Relevant business process
Assumptions
Business return given required changes
First list of candidate re-usable building blocks
Model of candidate building blocks showing inter-relationships
Organization sponsors
Project management structure
Plan for project phases
Meetings
The team shall meet ad hoc based on business need; Blue team change request, strategic business drivers, etc… 
Meeting documentation will be captured using 
Template - Meeting notes
.
Copy the template to the Red team meeting repository, 
Red Team - MT
 , using the following page title format, “Red MM-DD-YYYY - Meeting Notes - MT”.
The team shall collaborate and set the agenda ahead of meeting via editing/commenting on the upcoming meeting notes published in the team meeting repository.
A meeting scribe will facilitate filling out the meeting notes based on the discussions during the meeting.
Sub-pages under the published meeting notes shall be used to capture additional meeting artifacts.
