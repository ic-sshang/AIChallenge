The way our system handles unregistration differs across the platform. 
Customer Accounts can be unregistered from the Customer Portal, Agent Connect, Biller Portal, ICRestAPI or the RemoteAuthAPI. 
Our goal is to define the ideal consistent behavior for unregistration and apply it across the different endpoints unregistration can occur.

Biller Options

Inconsistencies
Blank Owner or Temp Login Creation
RemoteAuth and ICRestAPI sometimes create a new temp login post-unregistration
Group Oversight handling needs definition
Valid agent scenarios: when a payer is unregistering but an agent is to remain on the account
Error handling is not consistent
ICRestAPI is stricter and will fail if the owner login has no email address or is inactive
Customer Portal and RemoteAuth API do not have these checks in place
Customer Portal will send AutoPay OFF email notifications whereas RemoteAuth API and ICRestAPI do not

Ideal Unregistration Behaviors - Logins
 
 Non-MCL: Single Account Owner
If
 a Customer unregisters from their profile or a request is sent to unregister a Customer
Then
 the Customer should be unregistered entirely
And
 all payment methods tied to that User Login are deleted
And
 scheduled payments and AutoPay settings that are directly tied to those removed payment methods are unenrolled
And
 all email notifications are no longer sent
 
Non-MCL: Account Owner is Unregistering with a Co-owner
If 
a Customer unregisters from their profile or a request is sent to unregister a Customer
Then
 the Customer should be unregistered entirely
And
 all payment methods tied to that User Login are deleted
And
 all scheduled payments and AutoPay settings that are directly tied to those removed payment methods are unenrolled
Then
 the Co-owner on the account is promoted to Account Owner
And
 any account level settings (Paperless, Pay by Text) remain on the Customer Account.
Eventually we will offer Pay by Text at the user level. If the Customer unregistering is has a phone number, we should remove it and any associated Pay by Text settings.
