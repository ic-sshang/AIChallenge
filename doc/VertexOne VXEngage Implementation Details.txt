Update Email Address After Payment
By default, we would allow the owner’s email addresses to be updated in the payment route if the owner’s email address is empty (Allow email updates). 
Allow CC email updates:
 Update the CC email address when a different email is entered during the payment route. (Never update the main email address).
Prevent email updates:
 Prevent owner and CC email addresses from being overridden.
Allow unregistered updates:
 will update every time the customer is not registered.
The default value for this Option is: 
"Allow email updates"
