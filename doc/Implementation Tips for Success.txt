5 Criteria of success
Citeria
Description
1
Invoices
Know the “unique identifier” and be able to see it in the file. 
2
Adjustments
Ensure balances are being adjusted. Ask the Biller to validate. ​
3
Payments
Make certain all payments are successfully posted to the billing system.
4
Change logs
Verify paperless and auto-pay indicators are successfully transmitted to the billing system and/or print vendor. 
5
Presentment
Validate at least 3 sample bills which are included in the invoice file. ​
Conduct a comprehensive review (minimum one hour) of the template date-mapping. ​
Ask the Biller for real-world scenarios that may affect the presentment of the bill data (e.g. Budget billing, messaging)​
