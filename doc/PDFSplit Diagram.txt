Description:
This Confluence page provides a detailed overview of the PDF Split process within our company's new system, which leverages microservices architecture implemented through .NET C# applications. The process involves handling PDFs via Azure Blob Storage, ensuring efficient and accurate processing of PDF files.
Diagram Proposal:
The included flowchart diagram visually represents the PDF Split process, highlighting key steps such as PDF Identification, Splitting, Extracting Text, Metadata Linking, and various triggers. This diagram aims to enhance understanding of the workflow and facilitate improvements in functionality through containerization.
The diagram is intended to:
Illustrate the sequence of actions and decision points in the PDF Split process.
Provide a clear and comprehensive view of how PDFs are managed, split, and processed.
Serve as a reference for developers and stakeholders to ensure alignment and efficiency in the implementation of the new system.
