Summary
Invoice Cloud has two integrations with Sapiens, 
Sapiens GO for Workers Comp is real time integration utilizing EBPP and PDF linking for insurance. Web services are used for Invoices(BIF), RTDR, Payment remittances, Autopay/Paperless and Bill Presentment(PDF linking). Conveyance files will be transmitted through SFTP. 
Sapiens Coresuite (PolicyPro/BillingPro) is real time integration utilizing EBPP and PDF linking for insurance. Web services are used for Invoices(BIF), RTDR, Payment remittances, Autopay/Paperless and Bill Presentment (PDF linking using URL sent in BIF).
PolicyPro is connected to IC’s CloudPay for down payments and one-time payments.
BillingPro is connected to IC with a real-time EBPP integration for policy payments.
