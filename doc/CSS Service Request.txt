 Problem Alignment
Currently we don‚Äôt support service requests for starting or stopping services for utilities vertical. This functionality has been requested by several utilities billers and some insurance billers. The problem some of these billers face is that there CIS systems might support start / start service or policies but their payment processor does not. Therefore their payer experience when viewing their account or making a payment they have no indication of the status of their account. Therefore we have an opportunity to create a service request feature for requesting start or stop of service. 
¬†Objective
The objective of this project is to introduce a service request feature specifically for starting or stopping utility service into the GUB market. This aligns with InvoiceCloud‚Äôs goal to provide more tailored vertical specific options and aligns with phase one of our roadmap plan to build features for Enterprise Utilities.
