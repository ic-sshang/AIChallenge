Objective
Establish clear ownership for our 3 payment-focused Scrum teams to facilitate the Payfac transition, while maintaining the existing model during customer migration.
Furthermore, create a framework to support long-term growth, discovery, and continuous maintenance of the Payfac ecosystem.
Team Structure and Key Considerations
US Team
Tendies
: Focuses on payment operations and transaction processing, ensuring stability, reliability, and continuity in mission-critical payment flows.
Lead team for transaction processing, reconciliation, and funding—including complex areas like RMM.
Maintain and support existing systems like ICGateway (Hyperwallet, CASS, PayPal) until fully retired.
Experienced, US-timezone aligned, with deep expertise in payments, enabling fast incident response and proactive risk mitigation.
Strong stakeholder communication and collaboration across product, compliance, and operations teams.
India Teams
India Team 1 (Pace)
: Leads discovery and development for merchant onboarding and lifecycle management.
Drive forward the onboarding strategy and tooling for billers and sub-merchants.
Engage in Payfac discovery activities and API design related to KYC/AML, underwriting, and status transitions.
Continuity and ownership over onboarding aligns with the product manager’s ongoing discovery/design work.
India Team 2
: Specializes in risk management, compliance, and reporting, supporting discovery and development related to fraud, chargebacks, and compliance tooling.
Operating asynchronously with US, focused on regulatory adherence and reporting systems.
Contributes to Payfac and non-Payfac workflows via automation and monitoring systems.
