Payer Experience - Guest
 The payer will go to the biller/Horizon site and instead of logging in, will select ‘Make a Payment’. The payer will enter the policy number, mailing zip code and policy effective date to find their invoice and after selecting ‘RETRIEVE INFO’, the IC dialogue box will appear for a one-time payment.
Payer Experience - Registered
 The payer will go to the biller/Horizon site, select ‘Login’, enter their Username and Password, and select the ‘Make A Payment’ button. The payer will then see their policy information available for payment embedded within the All Points software and proceed through the standard Invoice Cloud payment process. They may also proceed with updating Paperless, AutoPay, and Stored Payment Options from this screen. 
Biller Experience - Existing Business Payment
From the Horizon All Points system (biller backend system), they will search the policy, select ‘Make a Payment Now’ when the pop-up appears, they will select ‘Continue to Make Payment’ and the IC dialogue box will appear tp make payment via the IC Customer Portal.
Biller Experience - Configure Payment Options
From the Horizon All Points system (biller backend system), they will search the policy, select the ‘Configure Payment Options’ button and complete the Saved Payment Methods action via the IC Customer Portal.
Biller Experience - New Business Payment (Pre-Policy Issuance) 
From the Horizon All Points system (biller backend system), they will search the policy, select ‘Make Payment’, when the pop-up appears, they will select ‘Continue to Make Payment’ and the IC dialogue box will appear to make payment via the IC Customer Portal Open Invoices Screen.
