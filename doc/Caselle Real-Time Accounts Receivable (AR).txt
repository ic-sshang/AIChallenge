Summary
The Caselle Real-Time Accounts Receivable (AR) integration is EBPP utilizing web services for all data transfer except invoice data (uses SFTP) and bill images (???).
 The integration utilizes Caselle Connect APIs for RTDR and Data Pump (payments and paperless change logs).
Data pump for ACH rejects, voids and autopay change logs is not yet available.
