Problem Alignment
The Problem
Billers using our Biller Portal often need to search for specific accounts, invoices, or payments. Currently, they have to navigate through different sections of the portal to find this information, which can be time-consuming and frustrating.
Why: How do we know this is a real problem and worth solving?
Based on feedback and support tickets, we know that billers struggle with finding specific information quickly. The lack of a central search function would address the common problem of navigating to multiple reports to find the requested information.
Audience: Who are we building for?
We are building this for our biller portal users. This feature will be beneficial for all billers, regardless of their technical proficiency or familiarity with our system, as well as our internal support team.
High-level Approach
By integrating a comprehensive search bar in the Biller portal, we aim to streamline the process of finding specific accounts, invoices, or payments. This search bar will update results as the user is typing.

Solution Alignment
Key Features
The search bar will be accessible from every page in the Biller portal, ensuring that billers can quickly find the information they need no matter where they are in the system.
The results will update in real time as the user is typing their search query.
The search bar will be able to handle multiple search terms and will highlight the matching terms in the search results.
Billers can filter their search by accounts, invoices, and/or payments using checkboxes. This allows for more targeted and efficient searches.
Key Flows
Using the Search Bar
Billers can access the search bar from any page in the Biller portal.
Billers can enter their search terms into the search bar. If they want to filter their search by accounts, invoices, or payments, they can do so using the checkboxes.
As they type, the search bar will provide real-time results, highlighting the matching terms in the results.
By clicking on a result, billers will be presented with available actions OR taken directly to the relevant account, invoice, or payment page.
