Intro
The Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) Team AAD Groups are intended to enable the 3 standard levels of access (Admin, Developer, Reader) to the Enterprise Data Warehouse Azure Resources.  In addition to the 2 standard levels of access (Admin and User) to EDW specific Jumpboxes.
Admin: 
ic-iac-datawarehouse-team-admins-{environment}
Developer: 
ic-iac-datawarehouse-team-developers-{environment}
Reader: 
ic-iac-datawarehouse-team-readers-{environment}
Jumpbox Admins: 
ic-iac-datawarehouse-team-jumpbox-admins-{environment}
Jumpbox Users: 
ic-iac-datawarehouse-team-jumpbox-users-{environment}
