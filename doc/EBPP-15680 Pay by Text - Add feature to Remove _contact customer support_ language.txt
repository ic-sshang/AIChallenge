At A Glance:
At InvoiceCloud, two billers can share the same BillerID. For instance, Western National Mutual Insurance (BID: 4038) and Franklin Mutual Group (BID: 3658) each have multiple underwriting companies that use the same BillerID. Typically, InvoiceCloud supports only one biller per BillerID, which necessitates modifications for billers sharing a BillerID. An example of such a modification was on the Pay by Text page within the Customer Portal. Billers with several underwriting companies often have distinct Customer Support numbers for each company. The requirement was to either display the appropriate customer support number for the specific underwriting company or to omit the number entirely. Ultimately, the decision was made to remove the number.

Repository Changes:
Two repositories had to be altered to remove the text from the Pay by Text page: MyIIS for the standard Pay by Text page and PayerPortal for the react version of the Pay by Text page. The text was simply deleted in both pages from both repositories. Note: the text had to be deleted twice for the REACT page since it was repeated twice: one for desktop and one for mobile. Because of this REACT pages must be tested in both desktop and mobile devices.
