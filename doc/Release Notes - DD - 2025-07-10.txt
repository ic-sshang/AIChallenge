Enhanced FTP Export Performance


Key Updates:




Added retry logic to database insert operations
 - This enhancement ensures that failed database insertions during FTP exports are retried automatically, boosting reliability and reducing the risk of data loss.


Improved error handling mechanism
 - Users can expect fewer interruptions during FTP exports, leading to a smoother overall experience. 




Enjoy the streamlined and robust performance enhancements in your database handling!
