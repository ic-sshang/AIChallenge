Overview
Custom Email Templates feature will allow biller to brand their email communication with custom field and design so that they can align with their compliance and custom requirements. Currently we are supporting template customisation but it's time-taking and not scalable as it's completely manual process. And, this will be self-service so it is going to reduce the requests from the biller for custom template.
Problems

User Context & Constraints
Who is configuring the feature?
The 
biller
 (typically an onboarding admin, or implementation specialist) is responsible for configuring email templates within the platform.
What are their goals?
Ensure all outgoing emails 
reflect their brand identity
 (logos, color scheme, tone).
Communicate in a way that aligns with their 
industry or vertical norms
 (e.g., insurance, tax).
Meet 
regulatory or compliance requirements
 (e.g., proper disclaimers, unsubscribe options).
Improve 
customer engagement
 and 
trust
 by sending clear, consistent, and branded communication.
What are their constraints?
No technical knowledge
: They are non-technical users with little or no HTML/CSS experience.
Time pressure to go live
: Email setup is one of the last steps before launch and needs to be fast and easy.
Legal and compliance restrictions
: Templates may need to include required fields, disclaimers, or unsubscribe options depending on the biller's industry.
Limited design skills
: Most users rely on visual cues and need WYSIWYG editing, not code-level control.
What motivates them to use this?
Ability to 
launch quickly and independently
 without filing support requests.
Flexibility to maintain 
brand consistency
 across all customer communications.
Enhanced 
customer experience
, leading to improved engagement, reduced confusion, and potentially higher payment rates.
Confidence that communications are 
compliant and professional
, especially in regulated verticals like insurance.
