Locate Files:
After cloning the repo there is some set up.
Search for 
.config
 in the solution explorer search bar and look for Web.config and App.config
Here are the paths to the files
ICRemoteLinking\ICRemoteLinking\Web.config
ICRemoteLinking\ICRemoteLinking.Business\App.config
NOTE:
 There is another Web.config under 
ICRemoteLinking\Views\
 please ignore it
Change Vault name value:
At the top of the pages there is a line 
<add key="VaultName" value="icappsettingsdevtest" />
 change the value to 
externalappsettings
 as shown in the image below.
Get URL: 
Get the PDF URL form the biller portal by right clicking the magnifying glass and click copy link
Paste the URL in a text editor and copy everything after 
https://www.invoicecloud.com/remotelinking
For example, from the URL below
https://www.invoicecloud.com/remotelinking/GuidewireAccelerator?InvoiceGUID=94B91F24-67E8-41DB-8104-A115C23E7092&LCT=qrpWLaM07xEgYwzt5QxLPpeM%2FMG%2BLLL4alHhxMcRe8Y%3D&BillerGUID=6f91d505-5e93-4bbe-901c-44d419f1566b
you will only select the following
/GuidewireAccelerator?InvoiceGUID=94B91F24-67E8-41DB-8104-A115C23E7092&LCT=qrpWLaM07xEgYwzt5QxLPpeM%2fMG%2bLLL4alHhxMcRe8Y%3d&BillerGUID=6f91d505-5e93-4bbe-901c-44d419f1566b
NOTE:
 The LCT is needed when running ICRemoteLinking and expires every 15 minutes.

ICRemoteLinking\Controllers\
 and integrations 
ICRemoteLinking\Integrations\
folders.
After setting breakpoints, hit play.
