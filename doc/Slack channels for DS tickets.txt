Purpose
Standardize the process for creating and managing Slack channels for DS ticket issues. The objective of these channels is to centralize all communications related to the issue reported in the DS ticket.
When to create channels
Required
All escalations. It must be created as soon as possible.
Any issues that require conversations with multiple stakeholders in different teams. (e.g. Requires conversations between Product Engineering, Dev Support, and CS)
Suggested
Any other scenarios where centralizing communications would be beneficial.
Process
Go directly to Slack, and find the Create Channel option.
In lowercase name your channel using the following format:
DS number, including the prefix.
Brief description of the issue.
Example
: 
ds-####-some-issue-with-xyz-service
Please add the channel as a comment in the DS ticket and state that all communications are now centralized in the channel.
Identify and add all pertinent stakeholders.

.
Clearly articulate what assistance is required from the teams in your first post to the channel.
Post updates as necessary
Archive the channel once the DS ticket has been resolved.
