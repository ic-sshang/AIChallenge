A page to provide some highlights of the comparison between the Identity Providers Auth0 and SecureAuth.
Why we are only comparing these two on this page?
We researched and compared various competitive Identity providers available in the Market to identify and compare the various attributes from those IDPs but based on the various features offered, Auth0, SecureAuth, KeyCloak and PingID are the ones which offer almost all those features and functionalities. 
We then ruled our KeyCloak because it is an open source solution and can have risks on the security aspects.
Based on the spike done by the PRISM team, we got to know that the PING ID is not very suitable with the .Net technology and the Auth0 and SecureAuth can be more compatible for our Biller Portal at Invoice Cloud.
