AutoPaySync:
When a customer’s account is enrolled in AutoPay, their bill will automatically be paid in full on the scheduled due date of the invoice.
The repositories listed below serve as the entry points for inserting data into the Data Pump table whenever an AutoPay is scheduled.

AutoPayMethodChange
:
While scheduling AutoPay for the first time, the customer must select a payment method (e.g., ACH or Credit Card).
If the payment method is modified before the invoice due date — for example, changing from ACH to Credit Card or vice versa — a new record will be inserted into the Data Pump table.
The sources listed below are responsible for inserting data into the Data Pump table during such AutoPay scheduling or modification events
