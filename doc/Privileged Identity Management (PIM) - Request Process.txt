Privileged Identity Management (PIM) Request Process
Requesting Access
 
Sign in to 
https://portal.azure.com
  with your Azure Credentials (the same ones you use to sign in to the VPN).
Search for Microsoft Entra Privileged Identity Management 
 
Click on “My roles”.
Click on Groups and activate an eligible assignment.
As of 1/10/2023, there are 4 available roles.
ICVM – The items prefixed with ICVM will grant the user RDP access to the servers. This is currently available to the Web servers, but will expand to the other server types. 
This will not grant admin rights.
ICDB – The items prefixed with ICDB will grant the user the same access level as a deployed application. 
This will not grant admin rights.
As of 10/1/2024, there is only 1 available role: ic-iac-avd-admins-npd
After clicking on “Activate”, a new blade will appear and ask for additional detail. Click on “Activate” to submit the activation request.
Required fields:
Duration: How long will you be needing access? Max is 4 hours.
Ticket Number: Jira Issue Id or Service Desk Ticket Id.
Reason: Short summary on why the access is needed.
  Optional fields:
Custom activation time: To defer the activation to a specific date.
Ticket System: Open ended, but we can state Jira or Service Desk.
On the top right, there will be a new notification stating that the request is pending approval.
