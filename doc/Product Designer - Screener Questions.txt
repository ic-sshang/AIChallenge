These questions should largely be answered yes/no, and help a recruiter pass/reject a designer for the next round.
Have you worked on mobile app design projects before?
Do you have experience with user research?
Have you used design tools like Figma or Sketch?
Do you have a portfolio of your design work?
Have you ever worked in a cross-functional team with developers and product managers?
Do you follow any design trends or blogs?
Have you designed for accessibility standards?
Do you have experience with prototyping?
Have you ever conducted usability testing?
Do you have experience working with brand guidelines?
