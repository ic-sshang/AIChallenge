Payment Sources
Payments can originate from several InvoiceCloud customer channels. Below are some of the payment sources that produce the most traffic.
AutoPay
 – Recurring scheduled payments initiated automatically.
Customer Portal
 – Payments made by customers via the web portal.
Shopping Cart
 – Payments submitted when multiple invoices are paid together.
Mobile Express Payments
 – Quick mobile-friendly payment option.
Internal Processing Systems
CloudTransactionProcessing (CTP)
Central service that ingests payment requests from all sources (except OBD).
Routes requests to the appropriate payment vendor for authorization.
Records transactions into the InvoiceCloud database.
ICPaymentProcessingAPI (PayPal/Braintree)
Handles requests from CTP for PayPal/Braintree transactions.
ICChasePaymentProcessingAPI (In Development)
Will handle requests from CTP for PayFac transactions (JP Morgan).
Payment Vendors
InvoiceCloud partners with several vendors for payment authorization and settlement:
Chase
 – Credit/debit card processing
Sage
 – Card and ACH processing
Braintree
– Online payments, PayPal transactions
BridgePay
 – Payment gateway services
Wells Fargo
 – ACH and card processing
TTech ACH
 – ACH processing
Bluefin (EMV)
 – Point-of-sale EMV card transactions
JP Morgan PayFac
