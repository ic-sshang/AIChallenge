Summary
Goal: Reduce slippage, increase quality, and identify risks.
How: Proactive, actionable and timely audit.
Audit is initiated by manager/team lead (not by the IM)
Review questions are aimed at ensuring project is progressing in accordance with the project schedule
Review is timely to allow for course corrections before needing to shift out the GL date
The recommendations are actionable
Risks are identified early, and confidence level is set correctly throughout the entire project (Expectations are set correctly with upper management and throughout the organization)
The review/audit is documented in writing and saved in the BOX in the biller's folder for projects provisioned prior to 07/01/2025. 
All projects provisioned after 07/01/2025 will have the Project Audit Checkpoint attached in Smartsheet to the project folder with a link on the Project Dashboard.
