Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
What is N.E.X.A. and how does it work?
N.E.X.A. (Natural Employee Extension Agent) is a 
generative AI-powered assistant
 designed to enhance back-office efficiency by providing 
natural language-driven data exploration
 and 
action execution capabilities
. Employees can interact with N.E.X.A. as they would a colleague, asking it to retrieve data, perform analyses, draft communications, and even execute entire workflows.
How is N.E.X.A. different from a traditional chatbot?
Unlike traditional chatbots that only provide information, 
N.E.X.A. goes further by taking action.
 For example, if a user asks for a list of customers not enrolled in paperless billing, N.E.X.A. can not only retrieve the list but also draft and send emails, recommend engagement strategies, and track campaign performance.
What types of tasks can N.E.X.A. perform?
Retrieve and analyze customer and billing data.
Draft and send communications to customers.
Automate back-office processes such as dispute resolution, account updates, and payment reminders.
Recommend and execute engagement campaigns to drive adoption of digital billing and payments.
Track performance metrics and provide insights on operational efficiency.
How does N.E.X.A. integrate with existing systems?
N.E.X.A. is designed to integrate seamlessly with 
ERP, CRM, and billing platforms
 used by utilities, insurers, and municipalities. It connects via APIs and can work alongside existing workflows without requiring major infrastructure changes.
How does N.E.X.A. ensure data security and compliance?
Security is a top priority. N.E.X.A. operates within 
strict compliance frameworks (PCI, SOC 2, GDPR, etc.)
, ensuring that all interactions with customer data are 
encrypted, logged, and auditable
. Additionally, N.E.X.A. adheres to enterprise-grade access controls, allowing organizations to manage permissions at a granular level.
