The following list provides the Jira Automation Rules that are currently in place for the Development Support (DS) project. 
Jira Automation Rules
Run Trigger
Trigger Detail
Description 
Set Scrum Team and Automation Labels
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8 AM - 5 PM CST
This rule will look for tickets unassigned tickets with the status “Pending” and pending reason “Awaiting Eng Support” and will set an automation label based on reported service. This rule will also set the Priority and the Scrum Team.
Assign Billing Me Softly Tickets
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8:05 AM - 4:55 PM CST
This rule will look for the oldest unassigned ticket with the label “Auto_BMS” and will assign them to the respective SMEs.
Assign Team SPAM Tickets
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8:05 AM - 4:55 PM CST
This rule will look for oldest unassigned ticket with the label “Auto_SPAM” and will assign them to the respective SMEs.
Assign Integrations Tickets
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8:05 AM - 4:55 PM CST
This rule will look for oldest unassigned ticket with the label “Auto_INT” and will assign them to the respective SMEs.
Assign Wicked Tendies Tickets
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8:05 AM - 4:55 PM CST
This rule will look for oldest unassigned ticket with the label “Auto_WICKED” and will assign them to the respective SMEs.
Assign File Processing Tickets
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8:05 AM - 4:55 PM CST
This rule will look for oldest unassigned ticket with reported services equal to “File Procesing“ and will assign them to the respective SMEs maintaining a balanced workload.
Assign Email Notification Tickets
Scheduled
Every 10 minutes between 8:05 AM - 4:55 PM CST
This rule will look for oldest unassigned ticket with reported services equal to “Email Notification“ and will assign them to product SMEs while maintaining a balanced workload.
Complete Canceled Tickets
Scheduled
Hourly between 8 AM - 5 PM CST
This rule will look for assigned tickets with the status “Canceled” and will attempt to complete the ticket. This rule will email the assignee if an affected service is not set.
Update WIP Limits
Scheduled
Hourly between 7 AM - 5 PM CST
This rule will update the variables that keep track of the number of assigned tickets for each member of the team.
Decrease Assignee WIP Limit
Transition
Ticket status moved from Work in Progress to Completed
This rule will decrease the value of the ticket assignee’s WIP limit variable by 1.
Remove Scrum Team and Automation Labels
Transition
Ticket assignee is removed
This rule will remove the automation label and the scrum team from the ticket.
Top Biller Peer Review Alerts
Transition
Ticket status moved from Open to Pending
This rule will assign watchers and email the assignee to peer review this ticket based on the Scrum Team.
Lack of Response 24 Hour Notice
Manual
Can be triggered by ticket assignee
This rule will have the automation post a 24 hours lack of response notice on the ticket.
Lack of Response Ticket Close Notice
Manual
Can be triggered by ticket assignee
This rule will have the automation post a ticket closure notice on the ticket due to lack of response.
Return Ticket to Assignment Queue
Manual
Can be triggered by ticket assignee
This rule will ensure a ticket meets the criteria to be picked up by the grouping automation and then be assigned.
Automatic Assign Based on Reported Service Q&A: 
Q: What if I will be out for more than 2 days? 
A: Let the team lead know and they will remove any team member that will be on leave/ out for more than  2 days from the Round Robin list upon returning the team member will be added back. 
Reported Service Q&A:
Q: What if the reported services incorrect? 
A: Correct the reported service and leave the ticket Unassigned > Pending > Awaiting Eng Support. Automation will remove the scrum team and automation label.
Q: What if the reported service is new? 
A: Let the team lead know about the DS ticket and the reported service so that they may add the reported service to the automation. 
Manual Trigger Automation Q&A:
Q: How do I trigger the manual automation rules?
A: Manually trigger automations can be trigger by selecting the automation in the Actions section of the DS ticket.
