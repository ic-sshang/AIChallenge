This document will guide in understanding the process to follow when a Biller/SP requests to meet any Support team member to resolve issues. 
Process:
Biller/SP reaches out to CS mentioning that they would want to meet with IC’s Support team to resolve the issue.
CS informs the respective Dev Support member about the meeting.
Dev Support member consults with their manager.
Based on the discussion, the Dev Support member provides their availability.
CS coordinates with Biller/SP and schedules a meeting.
For CS:
CS to articulate why a meeting is necessary and what is the objective that we want to achieve. 
CS must inform the Biller/SP the process IC follows to organize a meeting with the Technical team.
CS is responsible for communicating and arranging the meeting between the Biller/SP and Support team member.
For Biller/SP:
The Biller/SP is required to send an atleast 1-day prior notice for the meeting set with the Support team. To enable the Support team to onboard any other engineering team if required.
For Support Team Members:
Support team member must spend significant time to triage the issue and understand the root cause of the problem before meeting with the Biller/SP. (
By doing so the Support team member will analyze if additional engineering resources are required or not.
)
Who needs to attend the meeting?
Biller/SP’s POC/Support team
Client Service's team member who reported the issue.
Assigned IC Technical team member
IC Engineering resources (optional)
Meeting with Biller/Software Partner
