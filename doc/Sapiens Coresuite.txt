Summary
Sapiens Coresuite (PolicyPro/BillingPro) is real time integration utilizing EBPP and PDF linking for insurance. Web services are used for Invoices(BIF), RTDR, Payment remittances, Autopay/Paperless and Bill Presentment (PDF linking using URL sent in BIF).
PolicyPro is connected to IC’s CloudPay for down payments. 
Some billers may not use PolicyPro for down payments with IC.
BillingPro is connected to IC with a real-time EBPP integration for policy payments.
Sapiens Coresuite billers can bill at the individual policy level (balance forward) OR all policies at the account level (open invoicing). The invoices will be sent to InvoiceCloud accordingly (balance forward Y or N).
