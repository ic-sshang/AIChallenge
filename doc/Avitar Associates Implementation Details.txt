Dog License:
Instruction Text:
"Registered" 
means that you have already enrolled with this online service to review invoice and payment history, schedule automatic payments, or pay quickly with previously saved payment information.  If you have not registered but you wish to obtain a license for your dog, begin below by locating your pet(s).
TO VIEW AND PAY YOUR DOG LICENSE BILL:
Please enter your information into the fields below and click Submit to view the results. The Owner Name and Pet Name are  REQUIRED.
A current rabies vaccination is required for licensing. If it has expired you will be unable to renew a dog license online and must provide the paperwork from your veterinarian showing it has been updated.
Search Tip: For best results, for the Owner Name enter a partial name (for example, "John Smith" enter "John") and for the Pet's name enter the exact name.

Instruction Text: 
"
Registered
" means that you have already enrolled with this online service to review invoice and payment history, schedule automatic payments, or pay quickly with previously saved payment information.  If you have not registered but you wish to renew your vehicle's registration, begin below by locating your vehicle(s).
TO VIEW AND PAY YOUR MOTOR VEHICLE RENEWAL:
Please enter your information into ALL of the fields below, click Submit, and scroll down to view the results. 
Search Tip:  
The required search criteria below can be found either on your renewal notice or your current registration.
VEHICLE REGISTRATIONS RENEWED ONLINE ARE NOT VALID UNTIL you receive your decals and registration in the mail.
