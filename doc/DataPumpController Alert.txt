Description
DataPump Controller runs every minute on every app server to spin off DataPump.exe for each biller. The controller will basically pickup biller that have records to post in DataPump and update the processing bits accordingly. There are times where an error will come in when the job failed, this can cause delays in queuing billers or show signs of a potential issue.
Common Scenarios
Semaphores, mainly during Microsoft maintenance periods.
An app server was down or restarted unexpected during a job run.
Steps to Resolve
Check the logs to see what kind of error caused the issue. 
Based on the error, keep an eye on any issues with billers' DataPump. If so, a DP-Monitor alert will come in soon after.
If the job ended by a timeout, the next run will complete. But make sure anything other than a delay is accounted for.
