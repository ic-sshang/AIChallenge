Admin Role 
Manage payment details, view invoices, manage candidate attributes, and control your funding.

These folks can do 
most

Observer Role
Unlimited seats. 
If you want to invite someone to view research but not run their own studies, you can give them a free Observer seat. They'll be able to join calls and see your repository.

Add Availability
It is currently not possible to enforce availability across calendars that you do not personally have access to - even if those team members have connected their calendar to Great Question. However, any calendars that you personally have access to can be used.
Please see 
https://greatquestion.co/support/studies/scheduling-faqs#can_i_have_a_calendar_on_a_study_that_only_offers_slots_for_booking_that_are_available_on_multiple_calendars
