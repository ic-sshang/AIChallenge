Terraform Development Guidelines
Reduce the configuration burden to reduce the likelihood of misconfigurations between environments.
Minimize variable complexity.
Model purpose driven architectures within the terraform repos.
Encapsulate the complexities of the architecture.
Provide descriptions for all variables.
Provide examples, defaults, and validations for variables when possible.
Allow for easy extensibility of the architecture.
Enable immutable infrastructure deployment patterns.
