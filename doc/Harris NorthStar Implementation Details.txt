Rules of Engagement
There is a specific Harris NorthStar meeting bi-weekly on Thursdays at 1:30 Eastern.  NorthStar hosts this meeting.  Larry Roik hosts the call.  
LRoik@harriscomputer.com
 
There is a Implementation Document that is jointly completed by InvoiceCloud and Harris.  You will need to confirm who the Harris IM will be that you are working with during the bi-weekly meeting.  When you have completed the InvoiceCloud required area of the document please email to the Harris IM that is assigned to your project.  
Timeline
60-90 days to complete.  
In the past there have been implementations that have taken up to 6 months to complete.

Allow CC email updates:
 Update the CC email address when a different email is entered during the payment route. (Never update the main email address).
