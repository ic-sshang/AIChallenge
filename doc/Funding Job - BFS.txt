The funding job will send funding instructions to the funding processor to disburse funds from the funding source account to the target funding destination account.  
In the case of Hyperwallet funding instructions are transformed into entries in a OneBatch file.
In the case of Cass funding instructions are transformed into entries in a standard NACHA file.
