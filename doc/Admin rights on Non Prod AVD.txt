To elevate to local admin on your NonProd AVD for a max of 30 minutes at a time, please perform the following steps:
From your AVD, browse to 
https://portal.azure.com
 and sign in.
At the top of the page, search for PIM and open Microsoft Entra Privileged Identity Management
On the left side, select "Groups"
In the Eligible Assignments tab, you should see 
"ic-iac-avd-admins-npd". 
Click Activate on the far right.
In the appearing window, enter the reason and click Activate
After validation has been completed, you should be able to elevate your AVD within a few moments.
