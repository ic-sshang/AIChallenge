Purpose/Summary: Cloud SSO Connect is Invoice Cloud’s Implementation of a Single sign-on into the Invoice Cloud portal. This feature allows Billers and/or Partners to create a seamless experience for customers between their systems and Invoice Cloud, while keeping the scope of PCI compliance minimal for both Partners and Billers.
