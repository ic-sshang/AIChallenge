Inbound Accelerator Functional Overview
Guidewire Accelerator(PolicyCenter ,BillingCenter and Integration Gateway) is real time integration utilizing EBPP and PDF linking for insurance. Web services are used for Invoices(BIF), RTDR, Payment remittances, Autopay/Paperless and Bill Presentment(PDF linking).
Outbound Accelerator Functional Overview
Guidewire Accelerator(BillingCenter and Integration Gateway) provides disbursement functionality for premium refunds and commission payments  with payment modalities ( RTP, ACH, Cheque, Virtual Card, Debit Cards).
