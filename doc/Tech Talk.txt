Objectives
Promote Knowledge Sharing
: Encourage engineers to share their expertise and experiences, leading to collective growth.
Foster Innovation
: Create a platform to showcase innovative solutions and new technologies.
Enhance Team Collaboration: 
Strengthen cross-team interactions and collaboration.
Motivate and Inspire:
 Inspire engineers through exposure to cutting-edge practices and success stories.
Address Technical Debt: 
Highlight strategies for managing and reducing technical debt.
Benefits
I
mproved Skill Sets
: Continuous learning opportunities will enhance the skill sets of our engineers, leading to better performance and innovation.
Increased Engagement:
 Regular interaction and knowledge sharing will boost employee engagement and morale.
Enhanced Productivity
: Exposure to best practices and new technologies can lead to more efficient processes and solutions.
Retention and Attraction of Talent: 
A culture of learning and innovation is attractive to top talent and can improve retention rates.
Alignment with Strategic Goals:
 These events will align with our strategic goals of fostering innovation and maintaining technological leadership.
Structure and Implementation
Frequency and Duration
: Monthly 1 hr sessions (can be adjusted to bi-monthly based on need)
Format
: A mix of presentations, live demos, Q&A sessions, and interactive discussions.
Topics
: Diverse topics covering engineering innovations, new design patterns, project case studies, technical debt management, and more.
Presenters
: On a rotational basis, engineers from different teams, including offshore members.
Documentation
: Recording sessions and sharing resources (slides, code snippets) post-event.
Feedback
: Collecting feedback through surveys to continuously improve the sessions.
