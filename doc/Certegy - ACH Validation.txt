ACH Validation
During the payment process, our CTP performs additional ACH validation for specific payment sources.
Validated Payment Sources:
Web services
Jobs and Processes
Payment File Upload
Online Bank Direct
POS
Check21
IVR
Auto Pay
Scheduled Payment
Kiosk
Other Payment Sources:
 The remaining payment sources are validated in the payment route within the UI, such as Shopping Cart and Express Payments.
Biller Options
MonitorACHRejects (81)
Description
: Monitor ACH Rejects (81)
Function
: If enabled, IC will decline subsequent transactions made with payment methods (RTE/DDA/TYPE) that last resulted in a qualifying ACH Reject.
Qualifying ACH Reject Codes
: R02, R03, R04, R12, R13, R14, R15, R16, R20.
Impact
: Payment details will not be sent to the Payment Processors.
ScreenACHPmtMethod (77)
Description
: Screen ACH Payment Methods
Default Setting
: Enabled
Function
: ACH transactions will be validated against Certegy to determine whether payments should be allowed with the provided account information.
