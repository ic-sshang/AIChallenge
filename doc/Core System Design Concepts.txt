Systems must grow and change over time to continue to operate at peak capacity. Companies, people, and software are not static entities and there are many things that may precipitate the need for growth; business expansion, increased user demand, or even just evolving requirements. For this reason, it is important to grow an understanding of how to evaluate trade-offs when making architectural design decisions.
