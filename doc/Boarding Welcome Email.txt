Summary
This SOP defines the process for sending the Boarding Welcome email after the Kick-Off Call (KOC) and ensures that the correct stakeholders are informed and engaged.
note
Document


Document


Scope
This SOP applies to all Implementation Managers (IMs) and PMs (if assigned) and team members involved in the onboarding of new customers following opportunity acceptance.
Overview of Key Changes
The 
Boarding Welcome email
 will now be sent 
after
 the Kick-Off Call.
The 
Implementation Manager and/or Project Manager will be CC’d
 on the Welcome email.
The Welcome email will be 
triggered
 by a status change in the boarding case.
Process Flow
Opportunity Acceptance
Once the sales opportunity is marked as “Accepted,” a 
Boarding Case
 is automatically created.
The initial 
Boarding Case Status
 is set to:
➤ 
"Pending KOC" or “Pending IBR”
Kick-Off Call
During the Kick-Off Call, the 
Implementation Manager (IM) or Project Manager (PM)
 will:
Confirm the name and email of the 
signature authority
.
Enter this contact information into the 
Boarding Case
.
Updating Boarding Case
Following the KOC, the 
IM/PM
 will enter the following in the chatter of the boarding case notifying  the boarding rep (@[name]):
the contact details of the signature authority
confirmation that the kick off call is complete
  the IM will 
update the status
 of the Boarding Case to:
➤ 
"Pending Boarding"
Trigger: Boarding Welcome Email
Changing the status to “Pending Boarding” 
triggers
 the sending of the 
Boarding
 
Welcome Email
.
The Implementation Manager and/or Project Manager is 
CC’d
 on this email for visibility and follow-up.
