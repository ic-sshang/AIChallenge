Summary
Submit one request for multiple needs (Ex: bill presentment, implementation coding, Stratus install). This will be an ITG to be triaged. ​
Triage of each request will occur within 3 business days of submission. Triage completion includes providing an estimated completion date. This will change the ticket from and ITG to a Jira IT ticket. ​
Automation and data sync between Salesforce, Smartsheet and Jira Email upon submission.​
The Jira ticket will be one of the following depending on the request. SA – Solution Engineers, DBR – Data Base Request IT, DS – Development Support IT, EBPP – Product Request, or INT – Integrations IT.  ​
