Useful Queries:
SQL query to determine which BillerOptions where changed (Set to only find OptionID 9)
select ChangeLogID,ChangeRequestorDesc,ChangeDate, TableName,bo.OptionID,OptionDesc,OldValue,NewValue
from changelogs as cl
left join BillerOptions as bo on cl.fkid = bo.billeroptionid
left join tbloptions as op on op.optionid = bo.optionid
left join tblchangerequestor as cr on cr.changerequestorid = cl.changerequestorid
where bo.optionid = 9
and tablename like 'BillerOptions'
order by 1 desc
