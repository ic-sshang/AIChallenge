 Problem Alignment
Problem Alignment
InvoiceCloud currently has a confusing and inconsistent registration process. This limitation on our payer site prevents higher adoption rates for auto-pay, the metric linked most directly to retention and satisfaction. In order to compete in the industry, we need to update the UI and workflow around customer registration, which will result in greater adoption of online payments, autopay, paperless, and pay-by-text.
