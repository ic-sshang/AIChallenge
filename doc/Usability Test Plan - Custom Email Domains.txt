1. Test Overview
We're testing a new feature that allows billers to configure a custom domain for sending system-generated emails (e.g., payment reminders, confirmations, receipts) instead of using the default 
@invoicecloud.net
. This setup involves technical steps and coordination with an IT team. The goal is to understand whether billers can navigate the setup process independently and identify any usability or comprehension issues that could prevent adoption.
2. Objectives & Research Questions
Objectives
Evaluate whether billers can successfully begin the custom domain setup process using the prototype.
Identify points of confusion in the user interface, especially around technical requirements and collaboration with IT.
Assess how well the feature communicates the value, steps, and status of domain setup.
Uncover any blockers that would prevent a biller from initiating or completing the process.
Research Questions
Can billers understand the purpose and benefits of setting up a custom sending domain?
Are the setup steps clear and actionable for a non-technical user?
Do users understand what information they need to provide to their IT team â€” and how to access and share it?
Is terminology (e.g., DNS, SPF, DKIM, domain verification) presented in a way that is understandable or adequately explained?
Can users recognize what stage of the setup theyâ€™re in (e.g., ready to share with IT, pending verification, completed, error)?
