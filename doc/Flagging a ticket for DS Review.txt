The process we are putting in place now is to have a feedback mechanism to gather insights and flag tickets for internal review 
Any ticket that meets the below criteria can be flagged for DS Review
Tooling opportunity
Patterns of problems
Anything else that we would like to discuss as a team
When one of the above criteria is met. Label the DS ticket with DS_Review Label and add a note in the internal notes on the reason for CS Review
Sample Notes:
typescript
DS_Review: Daniel to review if there is a tooling opportunity
