Purpose
Standardize the DS ticket escalation process, and set clear expectations for Dev Support engineers and escalation stakeholders.
Escalation Roles
Initiator: The individual who escalates the issue. This person must be in a manager or lead role within their organization.
Contact: The individual from the client-facing teams who are appointed to be the available point of contact for the escalation. Usually the Reporter.
Reporter: The individual who reported the DS ticket.
Assignee: The Individual responsible for identifying the root cause and feasible mitigation actions.

Limits
General
No more than 3 escalations per day. 
No more than 5 active escalations.
Low-impact issues will not be accepted as escalations (e.g. One payer did not receive a notification).
Assignee
Only one escalation per assignee.
Will not provide completion estimates.

Process
The initiator will post the DS ticket with an articulated reason for the escalation in the #sre-escalations slack channel. The post must also include the Contact.
Someone in Dev Support will acknowledge and review the ticket. Once reviewed, an engineer will be assigned to work on the escalation. 
The Assignee must take the following clerical actions:
Add the ESCALATION tag to the DS ticket.
Move the ticket to the Highest priority.
