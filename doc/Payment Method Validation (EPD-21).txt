Problem Statement
#FFFAE6
Payers can register for accounts with saved payment methods already stored. Today, there is no validation in place to ensure the payment method belongs to the user registering for that account.
In our existing workflows the first user to login to an account with a stored payment method is considered the owner of that payment method. We do obfuscate the full payment details; however, we are not verifying that the user knows the information and should have access to that saved payment method.
Objective
Validate payment methods when a new user is registering for an account with a saved payment method
Value
Security
Secure registration process for accounts with stored payment methods
Enhanced trust & transparency on our platform
Lower risk for payment method errors or issues i.e., someone accessing a payment method that does not belong to them
Cost Savings and Revenue
Reduce support burden
Save costs by avoiding potential issues
More selling opportunities to prospective Billers with multi-user

Register the user and remove the saved payment methods
Notify user that AutoPay/Scheduled Payments will not continue, and a new payment method must be added in order to resume
