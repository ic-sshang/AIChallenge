Summary
Print Vendors/Software Partners or Billers who print the invoices/bills in house need to know who is signed up for paperless so that they do not mail out/print the invoice/bill. A print vendor or biller who prints in house if they put on the invoices/bills that the payer is signed up for autopay then they will also need to know who is signed up or have unenrolled. 
A change log will send only those payers who have either signed up or unenrolled from paperless or autopay for a given day. 
A full file will send over all customers in the billers database that are signed up or unenrolled for paperless or autopay.
Have a conversation with your biller: 
Is there a Print Vendor?
Is the client giving a file to the Print Vendor?
Is Invoice Cloud sending the file to the Print Vendor?
Print Vendors do not store invoice files; do they need a full history file of who is on Paperless or Autopay?
Does the clients CIS store all details (flags) of Paperless or Autopay customers?
