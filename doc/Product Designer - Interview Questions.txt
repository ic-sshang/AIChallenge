Answer
Portfolio Review
Organic Discussion
Design Thinking
Can you walk me through your design process from initial concept to final product?
How do you approach user research and incorporate findings into your designs?
Prototyping and Testing
What’s your process for creating prototypes? Can you provide an example of a project where prototyping was crucial?
What methods do you use to gather user feedback on your designs?
Have you ever tested prototypes? If so, what were you testing for and what were the outcomes of the research?
Collaboration & Communication
Can you talk through a project where you collaborated with other team members, such as developers, product managers, and marketers?
Can you give an example of a time when you had to resolve a conflict within a team?
What’s your process for addressing feedback and revisions that come from clients or stakeholders?
Can you describe a situation where you had to advocate for a design decision?
Accessibility 
What steps do you take to ensure your designs are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities?
