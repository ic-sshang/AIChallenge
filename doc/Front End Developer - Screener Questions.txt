These questions should help a recruiter determine if a candidate should move to the next round:
Do you have experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript?
Have you worked with front-end frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.js?
Do you have experience with responsive design?
Have you used version control systems like Git?
Do you have experience with CSS preprocessors like SASS or LESS?
Have you worked with RESTful APIs?
Do you have experience with front-end build tools like Webpack or Gulp?
Have you implemented cross-browser compatibility in your projects?
Do you have experience with unit testing frameworks like Jest or Mocha?
Have you worked in an Agile development environment?
