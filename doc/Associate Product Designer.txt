Job Description:
 
The Associate Product Designer is ensures that we give proper design centric attention to projects of all sizes. As an Associate, you’ll work with more experienced designers and product managers to execute high fidelity prototypes and UI refinements in projects of smaller scope, and assist on larger projects. Associate Designers work quickly in an agile development environment to ensure solutions are well defined.  
Responsibilities:
 
Create solutions for small focused projects and UI refinements.
Assist in the creation of wireframes, user flows, process flows, to effectively communicate interaction and design ideas. 
Participate in design team critiques to provide feedback and strengthen solutions.
Work within established product and design systems to create high fidelity prototypes.
Assist Product Managers in executing focused research tasks, such as user surveys.
Requirements:
 
Strong portfolio showcasing UX research and interaction design skills.
Proficiency in design software such as Sketch, Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, etc. 
Ability to solve problems creatively and effectively. 
Taking direction from Senior Product Designers and Product Managers.
Up-to-date with the latest UI trends, techniques, and technologies. 
Education in Human-Computer Interaction, Interaction Design, or a Visual Arts subject. 
Preferred Qualifications:
 
Familiarity with Agile/Scrum development process. 
Strong communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders. 
Ability to present your designs and sell your solutions to various stakeholders. 
Experience in creating storyboards, wireframes, and user flows. 
Experience working with design systems. 
What We Offer:
 
Competitive salary and benefits package. 
Flexible working hours and remote work options. 
Opportunities for professional growth and development. 
A collaborative and inclusive work environment. 
The chance to work on exciting projects that make a real impact.
