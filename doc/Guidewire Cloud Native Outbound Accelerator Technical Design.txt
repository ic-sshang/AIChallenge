Overview of the Accelerator
Purpose:
 Provides a secure electronic bill presentment and payment solution for Guidewire Billing Center for Premium Refund/ Commission payments.
Functionality:
 Processes electronic payments (ACH/EFT & Credit Card/Debit Card/Virtual Card / RTP ) and notifies Guidewire Billing Center on payment status.
