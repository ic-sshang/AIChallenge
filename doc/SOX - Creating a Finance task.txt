Description
 
Summary
The purpose of this process is to inform Finance that a Biller is live and that they need to audit the financial settings (fees, pricing, terminals, etc.,) so that there is one extra validation after Boarding did extensive QA before going live. This extra step is to comply with NACHA requirements and ensure accuracy.
This task also will be triggered by the Delivery team or when a change is made to a biller after going live, if the change impacts any financial of the client: fees, pricing, terminals, new invoice type.
