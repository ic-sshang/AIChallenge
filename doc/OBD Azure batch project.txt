 Problem Alignment
Due to the OBD processing being in a single job, each biller has to wait until the billers ahead of them are finished. The unmatched payments can sometimes take several hours into the morning before becoming available in the biller portal Pending Payments section. Ideally the billers will have access to these first thing in the morning.
High volume of OBD at the end of a quarter can already cause long processing times. Considering our number of OBD billers is ever increasing, this will eventually become a significant problem if it‚Äôs not addressed.
We‚Äôd like to run multiple instances of the application instead of a single job, which would reduce the amount of time to process the entire run. We plan to implement this in the same way we do Auto Pay (Azure batch).
¬†Objective
This will reduce the amount of time and number of errors that occur during the RPPS process, which will improve biller satisfaction by allowing them to match payments earlier in the morning. This aligns with the company goal of increasing OBD adoption among existing and new billers in 2023. It will improve performance and allow us to accommodate for larger billers that will be signed in the future without negatively affecting our other billers. It allows us to scale up the system as needed.
