Virtual Machine Host Groups
At InvoiceCloud we have 6 Groups (Pools) of Virtual Machines that server the host a majority of our Legacy Applications (which is around 95% of current InvoiceCloud applications).
The Virtual Machine Host Groups (Pools) are:
Web Servers
Responsibility: Hosting of InvoiceCloudâ€™s 3 main Applications 1) CRM, 2) Biller Portal, and 3) Payer Portal, as well as the APIs and other Services needed to support those 3 main Applications.
App Servers
Responsibility: Hosting of Background Job Processes (SkyBot Jobs) that accomplish a variety of Tasks.
Skybot Servers
IC-Skybot1 and IC-Skybot2
Responsibility: host the Skybot control / management plane, which orchestrates the jobs running on the App Servers
Proxy Servers
 Responsibility: Hosting of 5 WCF Services that sit in front of other Resources such as the Azure SQL Server or Azure Storage Accounts and act as a access gatekeeper and encapsulate certain functionality associated with interactions with the Resources they sit in front of.
Gateway Servers
Responsibility: Hosting of 3 APIs that are used by PSN for interacting with our systems.
PDF Extract Servers
Responsibility: Hosting of the PDF Extract Background Job Process, which extracts Invoice Data from .pdf files provided by our Billers.
MCC Servers
Responsibility: Hosting of the MCC Application and Services (a company we acquired that which some of our Biller base use to process transactions).
