Overview of Key Reports
SMART Disburse report
: This report will show all disbursements that have been created, the statuses of those payments (i.e., pending endorsement, completed, expired, etc..), and the vehicle selected by the recipient or the fallback method for expired payments, if applicable.
Funding Payment Account Daily Transaction report
: This will show all credits/debits into and out of the funding account. This will also show all debits (payments) out of the specific funding account along with the Bill Reference ID attached to the payment. This report also will show the beginning balance and ending balance for the specific funding account based on the date range entered.
Payment Account Daily Transactions report
: This report will show all credits/debits into and out of all the individual payment accounts. The individual payment accounts are linked to each individual payment that has been made. This report will also include the Bill Reference ID for each payment to tie back to SMART Hub.
