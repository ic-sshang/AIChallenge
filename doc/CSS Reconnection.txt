 Problem Alignment
Problem Alignment
Currently our GUB biller base must handle disconnection and reconnection of service manually or with another outside system. This creates more work for our GUB billers of managing when disconnection notices are sent to payers, organizing and tracking disconnection and then further helping payers make payments for reconnection. For example Town of Dartmouth handles disconnection notices completely separate outside ICâ€™s platform and then typically takes reconnection fee payments via phone or in person. This creates strain for the payer and more work for the biller to manage disconnection and reconnection. 
Further more we have Collier County who currently uses Cloud Store to require payers to make a reconnection fee payment to restore service. This is a work around and not an ideal flow for the payer or biller. Below is an example of the message. 
Therefore we have an opportunity to introduce a disconnection and reconnection feature to existing and new billers. This will help IC improve the billerâ€™s daily lives as we remove manual work, and support the payer experience to be online, reducing in person or phone interactions with the biller.

This will then help our billers. 
This aligns with InvoiceCloudâ€™s three year roadmap plan to support Enterprise Utility in the first year with customer self service and in app messaging features. We can market this feature as an add on to our existing product for GUB billers and we could also then collect revenue on more payments - ie being the reconnection fees.
