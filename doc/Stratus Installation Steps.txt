Download the software 
Go to 
http://Stratus.invoicecloud.com
  
Click on “Welcome to Stratus” on the left panel
Click on “Installation”
Then Download the latest Stratus Version 
Open Stratus.exe and install 
Click next  
Choose the location for the install, usually left on default and click next 
Click on install 
Click Finish 
“Add a new profile” Here you will be required to enter the GUIDs for the biller 
GUIDs are available in CRM → Stratus Management. 
Type the GUIDs and click authenticate 
Here you can verify you have the correct Biller and click Save 
Create the folders based on the paths of the tasks in CRM→ Stratus Management 
Now open Stratus 
Stratus will be found in the icon tray at the bottom right of the task bar 
Right click on the cloud and click on “Manage Scheduled Tasks” 
Here you would be able to see the tasks that are set up and we would need to run them manually to test. 
Right click on each task and run the task manually 
You want to see that all tasks have ran successfully under Last Result 
To confirm, go back to CRM → Stratus Management and you should now see HEARTBEATS and UPDATE PERCENTAGE 
Congrats, Stratus has now been installed.
