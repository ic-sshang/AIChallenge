Clear Browser Cache and Cookies:
 Sometimes, outdated or corrupted cache and cookies can cause CAPTCHA issues. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again.
Disable Browser Extensions:
 Some browser extensions can interfere with CAPTCHA. Try disabling your extensions, especially ad blockers, then refresh the page and try again.
Try a Different Browser:
 If you still have issues, use a different web browser. If you're on a Chrome-based browser, you could try Firefox, or vice versa.
Update Your Browser:
 Make sure your browser is up-to-date. An outdated browser can cause CAPTCHA issues.
Check Your Internet Connection:
 Sometimes, CAPTCHA can have issues if your internet connection is unstable or slow. If possible, try using a different internet connection.
