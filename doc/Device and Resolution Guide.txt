Objective
This page outlines the top devices and resolutions IC sees on our payer site, both on virtual site and customer portal. The goal of this document is to outline the top devices, browsers, resolutions we see in use today on our site and be a best practice guide for applying Pendo to the payer site at each respective device/ resolution use case. 
Pendo guides need to be made for each specific device type. This requires separate guides for mobile, tablet and desktop. Using Segments in Pendo allows us to create device specific guides.
