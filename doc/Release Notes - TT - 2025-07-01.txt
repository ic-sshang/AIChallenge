Release Notes 2025-07-01


Save Payment Source of a Transaction via Apple Pay




Enhanced Payment Tracking:
 The source of Apple Pay payments will now be saved, providing better traceability for users.


Improved User Experience:
 With the addition of payment source tracking, users can confidently review their transactions with more detailed information.


Streamlined Processes:
 This update allows for quicker access to payment history, making financial reviews easier and more efficient.
