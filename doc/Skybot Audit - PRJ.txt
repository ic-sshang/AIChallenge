Below is a guide to auditing the Skybot scheduling software.
Understand the System
Review: Examine the architecture, configuration, and job definitions
Identify Jobs: List all scheduled jobs and their purpose, 
Skybot Job Registry
 
Analyze Job Usage
Job Logs: Review job logs to see which jobs are actively running
Frequency: Check how often each job runs
Dependencies: Understand job dependencies (job a triggers job b)
Interview SMEs
Process Owners: Talk to process owners and users to validate job relevance
Business Impact: Understand the impact of each job on business processes
Recommendations
Retire or Optimize: Propose retiring or optimizing unused jobs
Documentation: Update documentation to reflect changes
