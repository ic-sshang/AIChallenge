Steps for Integrations when converting SSI eGov to SSI VIP
Steps for Integrations when converting SSI eGov to SSI VIP: 
BEFORE STARTING THE CONVERSION, please make sure that the biller is on a maintenance window before beginning. If the biller is not in a maintenance window, DO NOT CONVERT this biller. Also, please ensure that the IM has the new account numbers ready for Data Services to use to rewrite the old account numbers before converting over to SSI VIP.
