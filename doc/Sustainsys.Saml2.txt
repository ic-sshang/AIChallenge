About this library
The Sustainsys.SAML2 library is a .NET library that adds SAML2P support to 
http://ASP.NET
  applications, allowing them to act as either a SAML2 Service Provider (SP) or an Identity Provider (IdP). This powerful, robust, and adaptable library is widely used across a variety of applications for implementing Single Sign-On (SSO) and Identity Federation. Developers working with .NET languages like C#, 
http://VB.NET
 , and others commonly utilize Sustainsys.SAML2. The library, formerly known as Kentor.AuthServices, is actively maintained by Sustainsys AB.
