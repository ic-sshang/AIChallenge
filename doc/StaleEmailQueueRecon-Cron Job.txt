Description
StaleEmailQueueRecon 
A .NET console application that runs on a schedule to identify and alert on stale emails in the processing queue.
Primary Functionality
Identifies stale emails in the queue that haven't been processed within a defined threshold and sends alerts to internal stakeholders.
Key Features
Scheduled as a cron job
.NET 8.0 
Sends email alerts to internal teams
Simple logic with no endpoints
