Create MI:
Open Azure portal
Search for Managed Identity
Click Add
Provide the Subscription, Resource Group, and Name
Assign MI to Server:
Open Azure portal
Go to Virtual Machines
Bring up the server you want to add the MI to
Scroll down the main menu on the left until you see the Security group
Click on Identity
Click on User assigned
Click Add
Select your subscription and search for and choose the MI and click Add
Assign MI to Key Vault:
Search for Key Vault in Azure portal
Find the key vault to add MI to
Click on Roll assignments
Click Add
Choose the role to assign (standard are Reader, Key Vault Secrets User, and Key Vault Reader)
Click Next
Click Select members and paste in your MI name
Click Review + assign twice
