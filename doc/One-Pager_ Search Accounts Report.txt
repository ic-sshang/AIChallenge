Overview:
The Search Accounts Report is designed to meet biller needs by providing comprehensive reports on customer accounts. Based on an analysis of over 300 custom report requests in 2023, we have determined that a list of accounts is the most frequent request, accounting for over 60% of all inquiries. To fulfill this demand, the report generator offers a variety of filters and options, allowing users to customize their generated reports to meet over 80% of the use cases.

Key Features:
Flexible Search Criteria
: The Search Accounts Report allows users to search for specific accounts based on various criteria, including customer name, username, email address, account number, and invoice number. These fields support partial matches, enabling users to easily locate desired accounts.
Advanced Filters
: Users can refine their search by applying filters related to account status, email address, registration status, default payment method, saved payment methods, attention required, payment blocks, and balance due. These filters enable users to focus their reports on specific account characteristics.
AutoPay and Paperless Options
: The report generator includes filters for AutoPay, Flexpay, Recurring Payments, and Paperless settings. Users can choose to include accounts with specific settings or changes within a specified date range, ensuring relevant results.
Default Columns
: The report includes essential columns by default, such as Account Number, Customer Name, Email Address, Courtesy Email, Username, and Active Status. These columns offer a comprehensive overview of each account in the generated report.
Additional Columns
: Users have the flexibility to select additional columns to include in the report. These columns provide in-depth insights into account-related information, such as notification subscription status, registration dates, AutoPay details, Paperless status, payment method details, latest invoice and payment information, account balance, and more.
Benefits:
Time-Saving for IT
: The Account Report Generator reduces the burden on IT teams by providing a self-service solution for generating account reports. Users can easily access the necessary information without relying on IT support, freeing up valuable time and resources for IT personnel to focus on more critical tasks and projects.
Customized Reports
: With a wide array of filters and options, users can tailor the generated reports to their specific requirements. The flexibility offered empowers billers to obtain the exact results and information they need.
Implementation Considerations:
Implementing the suggested filters and options may pose challenges due to the extensive range of choices available. The feasibility of incorporating all filters and options will depend on the existing system architecture and development resources. Careful planning and development efforts will be required to seamlessly integrate the Account Report Generator into the existing infrastructure.
Conclusion:
The Search Accounts Report is a powerful solution that addresses the common need for account-based reports. By offering an extensive range of filters and options, billers can generate customized reports tailored to their specific requirements. This tool saves time, enhances decision-making capabilities, and provides valuable insights into customer accounts.
