Summary
QDS Integration uses RTDR for adjustments for utility, real estate tax, motor vehicle excise, and personal property.   Invoice file is sent once or twice a year through FTP.  Payment lockbox file is sent with batch close email daily.  No change logs are sent as biller pulls report through biller portal.  PDF linking is the bill presentment solution. IVR is also supported
