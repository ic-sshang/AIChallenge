AI Specific Metrics:
Intent Recognition Accuracy: Measures how accurately the AI identifies user intents.
Natural Language Understanding (NLU) Score: Evaluates the AI's ability to understand and process human language.
Sentiment Analysis Accuracy: Assesses how well the AI detects and responds to user emotions.
Context Retention: Tracks the AI's ability to maintain context across multiple interactions.
Learning Rate: Measures how quickly the AI improves its responses based on user interactions and feedback.
