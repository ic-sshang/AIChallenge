By Default, Autopay Reminders are sent 3 days before the invoice is due this means emails begin processing 3 days before the autopay collection date. 
Billers can change the number of days before the autopay collection date to send autopay reminders. 
Invoice Type Setting 63 - AutoPayment Email Reminders - Days before the collection date to send reminders.
Repo: AutoPaymentReminders 
Logs: 
Portal.Azure.com
 > iclogstorage> containers> icautopay >YYYY-MM-DD-icpaymentreminders > BillerID
Search by YYYY-MM-DD-icpaymentreminders for the day the autopay reminder was sent out or was supposed to be sent out. 
Note: Remember they are sent 3 days before the invoice is due so you will need to search by that date
