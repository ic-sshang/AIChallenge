Test Plan – Scheduled Payment Process Flow
To verify that scheduled payments can be enabled and modified successfully via the biller or customer portal, and that corresponding invoice updates and database changes occur as expected.
Test Steps:
Step 1: Enable Scheduled Payment
Enable a scheduled payment for any customer using either the 
Biller Portal
 or 
Customer Portal
, selecting either a 
future date
 or the 
current date
 for payment initiation.
Step 2: Execute Batch Job
Run the appropriate batch job to process the scheduled payment.
Step 3: Verify Results
Check if the invoice associated with the customer has been updated accordingly.
Confirm that the relevant records are populated correctly in the 
Data Pump
 table.
Step 4: Modify Scheduled Payment
Modify the existing scheduled payment details—such as the 
payment date
 or 
payment method
—and save the changes.
Step 5: Re-Verify
Validate that the modifications are successfully reflected in the invoice and database.
