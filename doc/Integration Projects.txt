High level summary of the process:
Request: 
New Platform (or Custom) Integration approved by Alliances/Sales
Alliance submits request to Integrations Engineering via Jira Service Management Request Form
Review:
Integrations Project Managers review request for required information/details
Move item to Backlog
Prioritize: Alliances, Sales and Integrations Project Managers review Backlog (by vertical) and prioritize
Schedule: Integrations Project Managers publish schedule of integration work and reviews with all stakeholders
Execution: Internal transfer, project kick-off, design, development, integration QA
Release: Integration certification and release/transition to implementations
