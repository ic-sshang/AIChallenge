Ledger Work
Incoming money shows as a positive amount in the ledger balance. (DSDR for Braintree)
Outgoing money shows as a negative amount in the ledger balance.
The ledgers represent the balances in the respective accounts.
For Braintree, money is stored in HyperWallet.
For OBD, money is stored in Cass.

Online Bank Direct
RPPS Entries
Incoming money for an incoming entry.
Outgoing money for a returned or reversed entry.
Payments
A sale payment is created when an entry is matched or processed as no invoice. Payments are then included in a funding batch to pay out.
A credit payment is created when an entry is returned or reversed. Payments are then included in a funding batch to debit the biller.
Sale payments records are not uploaded to gateway until the OBD batch close.
Outgoing money for sale payments.
Incoming money for credit payments.
Funding Batches
Outgoing money for a sale volume funding batches. (Linked to a Payment)
Incoming money for credit volume batches. (Linked to a Payment)
