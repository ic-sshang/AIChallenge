Add an Entra Group to Privileged Identity Management (PIM)
Go to the Group in Entra and in the 
Activity
 tab on the left select the 
Privileged Identity Management
 blade.  In that blade click the 
Add Group to PIM
 button.
Add a User to a PIM Group
Go to the Group in Entra and in the 
Activity
 tab on the left select the 
Privileged Identity Management
 blade.  In that blade click the 
Add Assignments
 button at the top of the blade.  
On the 
Add assignments
 blade:
Choose the 
Member
 option in the 
Select role
 drop down.
Click the 
No members selected
 link and then select the Users to add.
Click the 
Next
 button at the bottom of the blade.
Select the 
Assignment type
 of 
Eligible
.
Leave the default 
Assignment start
 and 
Assignment end
 dates giving the max of 1 year of Eligibility.
Note this is designed by Microsoft so Privileged Access Groups are routinely reviewed)
Click the 
Assign
 button on the bottom of the blade.
