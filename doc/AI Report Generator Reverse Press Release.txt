InvoiceCloud Launches AI Report Generator to Streamline Reporting for Billers
[Boston, MA] – [March 6, 2025] –
 InvoiceCloud, a leading provider of secure, cloud-based digital bill payment solutions, today announced the launch of its AI Report Generator, a groundbreaking tool designed to simplify and enhance the reporting experience for billers. This innovative feature leverages artificial intelligence to allow billers to generate reports using natural language requests, eliminating the complexity and inefficiency of traditional reporting systems.
Billers have long struggled with cumbersome reporting functionality that is difficult to navigate and time-consuming to configure. The AI Report Generator addresses these challenges by enabling billers to create customized reports for invoices, accounts, and payments with a simple text input, such as “Show me all overdue invoices from last month.” The system intelligently processes these requests and delivers detailed, accurate reports in a clear, readable grid format, complete with sorting and filtering options. This saves time, reduces frustration, and empowers billers to access critical data without wrestling with convoluted menus or complex tools.
Key Features Include:
Natural Language Processing:
 Intuitive report generation using plain language requests.
User-Friendly Design:
 A text box and example prompt cards to guide users.
Dynamic Reports:
 Grid-based output with headers (e.g., Customer Name, Invoice Number, Due Date, Total Amount) and options to sort and filter data.
Excel Export:
 One-click download of reports into Excel for further analysis.
Error Handling:
 Clear messages and suggestions for invalid requests or processing issues.
These capabilities make reporting faster, more flexible, and more accessible, encouraging billers to use reports regularly to drive better decision-making and operational efficiency.

FAQ
Q: What is the AI Report Generator?
 A: The AI Report Generator is a new InvoiceCloud feature that lets billers create reports using natural language requests, simplifying the process by instantly generating reports from plain text inputs.
Q: Who is it for?
 A: This tool is designed for billers, the end users who manage invoicing, accounts, and payments within the InvoiceCloud platform.
Q: What problem does it solve?
 A: It addresses the frustration and inefficiency of the current complicated reporting system, making it easier for billers to find, configure, and use reports without a steep learning curve.
Q: How does it work?
 A: Billers type requests (e.g., “List all payments from last quarter”) into a text box. The AI interprets the input and generates a report displayed in a grid, which can be sorted, filtered, or exported to Excel.
Q: Is there an additional cost?
 A: No, the AI Report Generator is provided at no charge to all InvoiceCloud customers.
Q: What benefits does it offer billers?
 A: It saves time, reduces complexity, and increases report usage by offering a simple, flexible, and intuitive way to access data, ultimately boosting productivity and decision-making.
Q: How does it handle mistakes or unclear requests?
 A: The system provides clear error messages with suggestions to help users refine their requests if something goes wrong.
Q: Can I customize the reports?
 A: Yes, reports can be sorted and filtered within the interface, and exported to Excel for additional customization.
