Transcard has the capability to support using a biller’s email domain for sending email notifications to payees, the 2 options are listed below. Please keep in mind that for both options, Transcard development is required. There will be an SOW between IC and the biller as well as one between IC and TC and additional costs may be incurred.
 
Option 1: SMTP - Client
Option 2: SMTP - Transcard
Option Description
Domain is owned by client (Client incurs cost per email)
TC adds the client domain to Transcard infrastructure. Their domain becomes part of our email domain and then we're sending through clients domain but using our connection
TC Actions
TC to authenticate to client's SMTP as provided by client
Add client's domain in TC sender filter in SMTP 
Provide to client TC public SMTP IP
Client Actions
Provide email server and credentials
Client server must use SMTP over port 587 (secure)
Register TC public SMTP IP's within their public DNS SPF records
